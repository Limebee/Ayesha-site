About Us

Our Founder had a vision of a salon that provides quality service to a wide variety of clients. They wanted to create a salon that catered to textured hair types. With Ayesha, this dream became a reality. We provide a variety of hairstyles and treatments. Our salon features skilled stylists dedicated to their craft. Our stylists have trained in cities such as New York City and Atlanta.
